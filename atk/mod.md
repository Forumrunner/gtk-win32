`<GlibEtcInstallRoot>..\..\..\..\..\..\gtk\$(Platform)</GlibEtcInstallRoot>`

`<AtkSeparateVSDllSuffix>-1.0</AtkSeparateVSDllSuffix>`

	*  add to `AtkDoInstall`

`<Import Project="..\..\..\..\stack.props" />`
